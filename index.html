About Me

I am a PhD student at the Centre for Research into Environmental and Ecological Modelling (CREEM) , part of the University of St Andrews .

My research focuses on developing and applying advanced statistical and computational methods to better understand and model ecological systems. I am particularly interested in environmental data analysis, population dynamics, and improving ecological modelling tools to support conservation and management decisions.

Research Interests

  • Statistical modelling of ecological and environmental data
  • Bayesian inference and computational methods
  • Population estimation and wildlife monitoring
  • Applications of spatial and temporal modelling
